Sherlocked

Enter the famous home of Sherlock Holmes and take on a thrilling and challenging heist! In the city center of Malmö, just a few minutes walk from the Central Station, you will find one of Europe´s top Escape Game Attractions - Baker Street by Sherlocked. For a thrilling 75 minutes you will be challenged in Sherlocks own home, meet several different characters and make it through an entire apartment from the 1800´s. Are you up for a challenging, thrilling heist at Baker Street?
